Why Choose Odoo ERP Software/open Source ERP

Odoo has been a strong contender in the open-source ERP category. Initially named as Tiny ERP and later renamed open-ERP, it was finally christened Odoo which reflected the multiple functions performed which are more than what a traditional ERP platform provides. These custom-tailored apps are off shoots of the already existing 30 core modules that are standard issue with Odoo.

Odoo differentiates itself by being simple and intuitive. Their ideology is based on the fact that business software should cover complex needs without being complicated. Their mission as updated on their website is 'to provide software that is intuitive, full featured, tightly integrated, effortless to upgrade, all while running smoothly for every business, every user. Some of the major reasons that make Odoo a competitive choice are:-

  • Open source

    As Odoo is an open source software that can be freely downloaded from the intemet there are no licensing fees or other hidden costs. It can be freely downloaded and modified to suit organizational needs. This ensures that the consumer has access to the complete source code.

  • Modular implementation

    Odoo is able to offer a high degree of customization to organizations. It can be implemented module-wise allowing organizations to have leaner ERP's and implement required modules in a phased manner. This lowers the cost of implementation and more importantly it incentivizes smaller organizations to implement Odoo at an earlier stage and later upgrade their modules when required.

  • Hosting flexibility

    Odoo can be hosted on-site or on a cloud platform giving more leverage and cutting down costs. This saves time, manpower and infrastructure directed towards hosting it on a server.

  • Customization

    Since an ERP will integrate with a host of essential functions like accounting, sales, production, warehousing etc, it is very important that the platforms are customized to suit the organizational needs. Being an open-source software, customization is one of the biggest strengths of Odoo ERR A custom tailor-made ERP is better placed to support an organization and improve its efficiency.

  • Low cost of ownership

    One of the essential factors that is highly considered in choosing an ERP software is its cost of operation and implementation per user. Since Odoo is an open-source software, it can be scaled up to accommodate new users with zero cost. Funds can therefore be directed towards effective customization and implementation.

  • Easy migration

    Odoo allows for easier and efficient migration with already existing ERP systems paving the way for a unified & centralized ERP system which makes administration and management very intuitive and easy.

  • EIntegrated Information

    Collating data from multiple sources and directories into a centralized database makes it easier to generate insights and help in decision making. Data is also kept consistent and up to date facilitating a dynamic organization.

  • Reporting

    ERP software makes reporting easier and more customization. With improved reporting capabilities, your organization can respond to complex data requests more easily. Employees can also run their own reports from the system improving accessibility of data.

  • Flexible and Comprehensive

    ERP software's require a high level of integration with your organization which mandates that the software should be highly flexible to give a perfect fit. There cannot be any compromise on functionality and adaptability of the ERP in line with your business functions and processes

    With over 1000 modules available on the Odoo market and new ones being developed almost every-day, the Global developer network is one of the most comprehensive networks that takes care of evolving needs of your business.

  • Efficiency

    An ERP system streamlines business processes, eliminating repetitive processes & reduces the need for manual data entry. Processes like approvals, requests, queries can be processed faster.

  • Vibrant Community

    The biggest strength that Odoo has lies in its dedicated community that is actively developing new apps for use by the community. ERP systems are a long-term investment with a considerably long time-period of application. Having a dedicated support staff adds to the long-term evaluation of Odoo. The community also serves as a measure of trust that has been garnered by Odoo.
