Implementation Services in D365

Microsoft Dynamics 365 is an intelligent business applications suite (ERP and CRM) that helps run your entire business and deliver greater results through predictive, AI-driven insights. It allows organizations to see business and customer data in one place, share that data across business lines, and enable a more proactive approach to accelerate business results.

With tools for finance, operations, human resources, sales, marketing, customer service, and more, Microsoft Dynamics 365 offers end-to-end applications to help you unify data and relationships, build intelligence into your decision making, and achieve better results

Services Detail:

  • Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance

    Connect & manage clients, finances, projects, and people in a unified, integrated environment. Get the right financial insights, drive corporate strategy & growth, decrease debt through efficient collection management. Discover hidden sources of cash & identify the profitable customer through rich visualization.

  • Microsoft Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management

    Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management empowers employees and organizations with the ability to obtain a unified view of inventory, warehouse, manufacturing, service, and logistics with predictive analytics (using AI and IoT) that turn data into insights to support better strategic decisions and maximize operational efficiency, product quality, and profitability.

  • Microsoft Dynamics 365 Commerce

    Connect physical and digital stores, track customer behaviors and requirements, deliver personalized experiences, and engage with your customers seamlessly across channels. Dynamics 365 Commerce is an intelligent solution that creates rich, customer-centric experiences at all touchpoints and connected to back-end operations.

    Dynamics 365 Commerce provides seamless merchandising, inventory, order management, warehousing, financials, AI-driven personalization & fraud protection along with eCommerce experience.

  • Microsoft Dynamics 365 Project Operations

    Dynamics 365 Project Operations brings the capabilities from Project Service Automation (PSA), Microsoft Projects and Microsoft Planner.Dynamics 365 Project Operations connects your sales, resourcing, project management, and finance teams within an available application to win more deals, accelerate delivery, empower employees, and maximize profitability

  • Microsoft Dynamics 365 Human Resources

    Manage entire lifecycle from recruitment to retirement and efficiently deliver highly professional service. Balance operational excellence, organizational agility, and the employee experience to create a workplace where people and the business succeed

  • Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central

    Make well-versed decisions with associated data across customer transactions, inventory, purchasing, sales, and accounting. Measure and optimize resources capacity, improve financial visibility, optimize supply chain, boost sales, deliver projects within budget, and safeguard your data with Business Central.Business Central features an intuitive, easy-to-use interface to help organizations get a clear overview of their business
