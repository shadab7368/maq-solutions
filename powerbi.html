Explore and do more with Power BI

Microsoft Power BI is a business analytics solution that helps you to produce quick visualizations, and reports, while having the option to share them with colleagues, on the web and across mobile devices. Power BI help you to access data from hundreds of supported on-premises and cloud-based sources:

  • Dynamics 365 and Dynamics 365 Business Central
  • Azure SQL DB
  • Excel
  • SharePoint and many others

Now, the tightened integration with Microsoft 365 products aims to streamline collaborative report building and made sharing datasets, reports, providing extensive modeling and real-time analytics for group projects, divisions, or the entire corporation.
